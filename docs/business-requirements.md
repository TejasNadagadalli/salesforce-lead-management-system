# Business Requirements

## Objective

To automate lead assignment and improve lead management efficiency for sales teams operating across multiple regions.

## Business Problems

- Manual lead assignment delays
- Inconsistent follow-up process
- Poor lead visibility
- Missing customer information
- Lack of reporting insights

## Proposed Solution

The system automates lead routing, enforces validation rules, sends assignment notifications, and provides dashboards for monitoring lead performance.

## Expected Outcomes

- Faster lead assignment
- Improved response times
- Better data quality
- Increased sales visibility
- Improved operational efficiency
